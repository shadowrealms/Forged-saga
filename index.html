Why the House of Mercy Matters

Stories have always shaped the world long after the people who created them are gone. The reason we write books and develop films at GE & LE Studios is not simply to entertain. It is to build something that outlives us.

At the center of that vision is the House of Mercy. Creative work can do more than generate attention or profit — it can generate sustained good. A place that feeds the hungry. A place where those who have fallen through the cracks are treated with dignity. A place where creativity funds compassion.

The goal isn’t legacy for the creators. The goal is continuity for the mission.
